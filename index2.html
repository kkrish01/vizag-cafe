Our Story

A Taste of Tradition,
Crafted with Passion

Born from a love for authentic South Indian cuisine, Vizag Cafe brings the vibrant flavors of coastal Andhra and Tamil Nadu to the heart of Raipur. Every dish is a celebration of tradition — from hand-ground spices to freshly fermented batter.

We believe great food brings families together. Whether you're craving a crispy masala dosa for breakfast or a comforting bowl of tomato soup for dinner, Vizag Cafe is your home away from home.
